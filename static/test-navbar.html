Dark Mode Support

The navbar responds to your system's prefers-color-scheme setting:

  • Light mode: Black text on white background
  • Dark mode: Light gray text (#dddddd) on dark background (#1b1b1b)

Try changing your system theme to see the navbar adapt automatically.

Responsive Design

On screens 540px or narrower, the navbar switches to a hamburger menu. Try resizing your browser window!
