Chess players are infuriating. Trying to organise them is like trying to herd cats. Trying to converse with them is like trying to draw blood from a stone. For a bunch of people capable of playing an immensely complex and nuanced game to a high level, they are almost universally idiots.

I am absolutely one of said idiots.
