--- aliases: - /mendokusai/2017/11/03/failure-vs-regret author: Jonny Spicer type: blog blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4848445585442664375.post-1026153144442383128 blogger_orig_url: http://jonnyspicer.blogspot.com/2017/11/failure-vs-regret.html date: "2017-11-03T05:49:00Z" modified_time: "2017-11-03T05:49:24.732-07:00" categories: null title: Failure vs regret --- I am constantly scared of failure, even if I know it is irrational. I find it very easy to be consumed by this fear of failure, and often find myself feeling down when small things go wrong. But I know that the real fear should be of regret, as the pain of regret is far worse than the pain of failure. While I worry day to day about whether or not I will try and fail, I ought to remember that to not try at all would be disastrous.
